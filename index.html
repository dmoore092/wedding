Our Story

We first met in November, 2011, when Dale came to the rescue for Sam while she was interpreting a tough phone call at work. Although both of us were working interpreters at Sorenson and in the community we had never met. After a quick introduction we worked together for over an hour before Dale was done for the day. A half hour later Sam was able to take a break, only to find Dale still there and talking with our boss (colleague and friend) Jo Ellen. Sam yelled into the office “He’s awesome!” and ran off to take her break. Sam later found out that he was in there talking with Jo Ellen about her! Jo Ellen played match-maker, and soon after we began dating.

Flash forward 6 years later, we were engaged on December 1, 2018 overlooking Seneca Lake. We have happily shared a home together with our 2 cats, Sadie and Leah, since June, 2015. We both share a love of travel and enjoy staying in AirBnB’s and experiencing the local culture of our destination. We have traveled to Puerto Rico multiple times, the Dominican Republic twice, and had an amazing European adventure to Germany, Poland, and Italy in 2016.

We look forward to sharing our Wedding Week with our guests in La Romana March 7-14, 2020!
